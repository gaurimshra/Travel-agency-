Travel Tips

Make your trip smoother and safer with these quick tips:

  • Always carry photocopies of your ID proofs and keep the originals safe.
  • Keep some local currency with you for small shops and emergencies.
  • Check the weather forecast and pack according to the season.
  • Buy a basic travel insurance plan for medical and luggage safety.
  • Keep digital backups of tickets, hotel bookings and travel documents.
